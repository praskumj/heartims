About HeartIMS

A social, safe and simple movement system

HeartIMS (Heart Interactive Movement System) solves a key failure of solitary fitness apps: low engagement. By turning movement into collaborative team play and gentle competition, HeartIMS makes exercise social, motivating and repeatable. The platform is built for older adults and care settings — prioritizing zero-friction access, clinical safety, and measurable preventative outcomes.

HeartIMS — How it works

AI-powered, controller-free activity for everyday living spaces

Designed for adults — accessible and low-friction

HeartIMS uses AI-powered body motion tracking to transform any living room into an activity zone — no wearables, no controllers. Setup is plug-and-play: connect your phone to the TV and start. Content includes gentle exercise routines, balance games, and brain-training activities tailored to mobility and cognitive goals.

Social by design

The platform encourages group participation and cooperative play through "Team Play" modes and light competition. Group sessions increase motivation, reduce isolation and create social bonding opportunities for adults, family members and care-facility groups.

Controller-Free

AI motion tracking — no wearables or extra hardware required.

Simple Setup

Plug-and-play connection to TV via phone — minimal configuration.

Team Play

Cooperative games, gentle competition and social sessions to keep users returning.

Peace of Mind

Clinical safety, institutional trust, and risk reduction

Medical Rigor

HeartIMS integrates official doctor approved heart health protocols to provide immediate clinical credibility. Content and thresholds are aligned with accepted guidelines so caregivers and institutions can trust activity prescriptions.

Physiological Safety Zone

Real-time heart-rate sensor integration (optional wearable) defines a Physiological Safety Zone. If a user's heart rate spikes, the system automatically reduces game intensity and notifies caregivers, reducing acute risk during activity.

Preventative Outcomes

HeartIMS supports fall prevention, mobility maintenance and mental engagement. Dual-task activities promote cognitive health and neuroplasticity, addressing health priorities in cognitive and mental health while supporting physical resilience.

Institutional Readiness

Designed for adults, children, assisted living and care facilities — HeartIMS includes administrator controls, session reporting and secure data flows to integrate with existing care workflows and research initiatives.
